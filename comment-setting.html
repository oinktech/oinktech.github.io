Privacy Policy

We welcome all relevant and respectful comments. To ensure that our comment section remains friendly and constructive, please adhere to the following guidelines:

1. Respect Others

Please respect the viewpoints and opinions of other users and maintain constructive discussions.

2. Prohibited Content

Comments containing profanity, hate speech, or any form of derogatory content are strictly prohibited. Such comments will be deleted immediately.

3. Compliance with Law

All comments must comply with applicable laws and regulations. Any illegal content will be removed, and relevant information may be reported to the authorities.

4. Personal Privacy

Please do not disclose personal sensitive information, such as contact details or addresses, in your comments to protect your privacy.

5. Editing Rights

We reserve the right to edit or delete any comments to ensure the quality and safety of the comment section.

Thank you for your support and understanding. If you have any questions or need to report inappropriate comments, please contact our support team.
